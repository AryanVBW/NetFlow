The Short Version

NetFlow is an open-source, local-only Android application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, network traffic, browsing history, or any personally identifiable information. All processing happens exclusively on your device. We have no backend servers. We cannot see your data because we never receive it.

1. Overview & Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the NetFlow Android application ("the App"), published by Aryan Vivek Bhardwaj ("we," "us," or "our"). It explains how the App handles data in connection with its core functionality as a local, on-device network traffic monitor.

This policy is designed to meet the requirements of the Google Play Store Developer Program Policies, including the User Data policy, as well as applicable data protection regulations including GDPR and CCPA.

3. Data Processed Locally On-Device

To deliver its core features, NetFlow processes certain data strictly on your device, in memory only. This data is never written to persistent external storage and never transmitted off your device.

VpnService

Local Network Traffic Interception

NetFlow uses the Android VpnService API to establish a local loopback VPN tunnel. This intercepts TCP and UDP connection metadata (destination IP, port, protocol, and byte counts) to provide per-app traffic statistics and risk scoring. We do not forward your traffic to any remote proxy server. We do not decrypt TLS/SSL-encrypted payloads. All data is processed in-memory in real time and discarded immediately after display.

QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES

Installed Application Mapping

NetFlow requires the QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES permission to attribute each network connection to the specific application that initiated it (by mapping the UID of the connection to a package name). This is essential to the core functionality of showing per-app network activity. This list of applications is used exclusively for on-screen display and is never transmitted off your device, stored to disk, or shared with third parties.

INTERNET

Network Access

The standard Internet permission is required by the VpnService infrastructure and for checking update availability from the public GitHub Releases page. No personal data is attached to any such requests.

RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED

Auto-start on Boot (Optional)

If enabled by the user, this permission allows the monitoring service to resume automatically after a device reboot. This feature is opt-in and can be disabled in Settings at any time.

FOREGROUND_SERVICE

Background Service

To monitor traffic continuously when the app is not in the foreground, NetFlow runs as an Android Foreground Service with a persistent notification. This is required by Android OS policy for apps that perform ongoing background work. User can stop the service at any time from the notification or app UI.

4. Third-Party Services & SDKs

NetFlow does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs (e.g., Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Mixpanel), advertising SDKs, or social login providers. The App has no embedded third-party trackers.

The App may connect to the public GitHub API (api.github.com) solely to check for new release versions. This request does not include any personal or device data beyond a standard HTTP request.

6. Data Security

Because no personal data is transmitted to or stored by us, the risk of a server-side data breach affecting your information is zero. All data processed by NetFlow remains exclusively within the Android OS security sandbox on your device, subject to Android's standard application isolation model.

7. Children's Privacy

NetFlow is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum digital age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because we do not collect any user data whatsoever, there is no specific risk relating to children's data from the use of this App.

8. Your Rights & Data Deletion

Because NetFlow does not collect or store your personal data on any external server, there is no personal data held by us that could be subject to a deletion or access request. All data the App uses is processed exclusively on your device and can be cleared at any time by:

  • Clearing the App's data via Android Settings → Apps → NetFlow → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the application
