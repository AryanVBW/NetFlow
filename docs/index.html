Core Features

Per-App Analytics

Map every byte of data and every DNS request back to the exact app that made it — in real time.

Local VPN Proxy

Uses Android's VpnService as a loopback proxy. Your traffic never leaves your device or routes to remote servers.

Risk Prediction

On-device algorithms flag suspicious connections, unknown ports, and known tracker endpoints.

Ad & Tracker Blocking

Block ads and trackers at the DNS and connection level without needing root access.

Bandwidth Monitoring

View upload and download statistics per app, per session, or across time ranges.

How It Works

Privacy First Design

NetFlow establishes a local VPN tunnel entirely within your device. No traffic is forwarded to any external server. All analysis is done in-memory and discarded immediately after display.

Modern Android devices run dozens of apps that continuously transfer data in the background. Without an on-device monitor, there is no way to know if an app is contacting advertising or surveillance infrastructure.

NetFlow leverages the Android VpnService API to read TCP/UDP connection headers without decrypting TLS payloads. This design keeps your browsing content private while revealing which destinations your apps communicate with, their data volumes, and their risk scores.
